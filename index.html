Sunshine Coast Itinerary

Explore Gibsons, Sechelt, Roberts Creek & more!

Day 1 - 17th April 2025 (Thurs): Arrival & Exploring Gibsons

12:35 PM - 1:00 PM: Arrive at Langdale on the ferry. Drive to Gibsons (10-15 minutes).

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch at Uptown Eatery

2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Check in to Gibson Garden Hotel and Visit Gibsons Public Market & Harbor.

4:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Explore Gibsons Landing (shops, cafes, galleries). *** Collect Cake ***

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Dinner at Molly’s Reach (seafood, comfort food).

8:00 PM: Stroll at Armours Beach.

Day 2 - 18th April 2025 (Fri) : Sunshine Coast Area & Relaxation

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM: Drive to Sechelt (30 minutes).

10:00 AM - 12:00 PM: Sechelt Waterfront (Trail Bay Pier)

12:00 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch at Lighthouse Marine Pub

1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Visit Porpoise Bay Provincial Park.

3:30 PM - 5:30 PM: Visit Sunshine Coast Botanical Garden

5:30 PM - 7:00 PM: Dinner at Kien Giang Restaurant

7:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Return to Gibsons.

Day 3 - 19th April 2025 (Sat): Explore Roberts Creek & Relaxing Beach Walks

9:00 AM - 11:00 AM: Visit Robert Creeks Pier and Roberts Creek Mandala

11:00 AM - 12:30 PM: Drive to Ɂiyuls (Cliff Gilker) Park

12:30 PM - 2:00 PM: Lunch at The Wobbly Canoe (Davis Bay)

2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Walk around Davis Bay Pier

4:00 PM - 6:00 PM: Head back hotel to chill

6:00 PM - 8:00 PM: Dinner at Friendly Thai Restaurant or Won Japanese Restaurant

Day 4 - 20th April 2025 (Sun): Secret Beach & Relaxed Exploration

8:30 AM - 10:30 AM: Breakfast/Chill/Check-out

10:30 AM - 11:30 AM: Visit Secret Beach Park

11:30 AM - 12:30 PM: Explore Lower Gibsons for Shopping or Relaxing.

12:30 PM - 1:30 PM: Lunch at The 101 Brewhouse

1:30 PM - 2:15 PM: Head to Langdale Ferry Terminal for 3:15 PM ferry
